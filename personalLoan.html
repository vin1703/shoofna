Features of Personal Loan

A Personal Loan is an unsecured loan that is not backed by collateral or security. This makes it a flexible financing option, as there are no limitations on its use.

The followings are the ways a personal loan can be useful:

  • Loan Amount: Up to Rs.40 Lakh
  • Flexible Tenure: Up to 84 Months
  • No Guarantor/Collateral Required
  • Transparent Process: No Hidden Charges

Benefits of Personal Loan

A personal loan is an unsecured loan that provides financial flexiblity for various needs including:

The followings are the ways a personal loan can be useful:

  • Debt Condolidation: Merge multiple debts into a single,manageable loan.
  • Home Renovation: Improve your living space and enhance property value.
  • Medical Energencies: Cover unexpected healthcare expenses.
  • Unplanned Expenses: Manage urgent financial need without hassle.

Personal Loan Eligibility

  • Age: 21 to 60 year
  • Minimum Income: 15000/month(salaried)
  • Credit score 700+
  • Stable Employment: Minimum Required work experience
  • Resident Citizen of India
